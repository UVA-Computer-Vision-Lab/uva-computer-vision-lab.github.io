Textbook


This course does not require any textbooks. The field of 3D computer vision is moving fast -- our major reading materials are research papers published at main computer vision conferences such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, and 3DV. The following textbooks are great references:

Multiview Geometry in Computer Vision

Authors: Hartley and Zisserman

Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications, 2nd ed.

Authors: Richard Szeliski

Pre-requirements


This courses assume basic knowledge in Linear Algebra, Optimization, and Deep learning. In particular, students should be familiar with topics including,

  • Linear Algebra: matrix multiplication and inversion, rank, Sigular Value Decomposition (SVD), etc.
  • Numeric Optimization: iterative optimization (e.g. SGD), constrained optimization
  • Deep Learning: common neural network architecture (e.g., MLP, ConvNets, Transformer), loss functions, optimizers, regularization, etc.

Course Policies


  • Free Late Days: You have 7 late days to use, with a maximum of 3 days per assignment. After using all 7, each additional day incurs a 25% penalty. Assignments exceeding 3 late days won’t be accepted. Late day calculations are based on the latest submission on Gradescope; even a minute late counts as one full day.
  • Documented Late Days: Additional late days require documentation as per university policy (e.g., a doctor's note).
  • Undocumented Late Days: You may justify late submissions in your assignment. These justifications will be reviewed at the semester's end, and penalties may be waived at the instructor’s discretion.
  • Academic Honesty: Copying from any source or sharing solutions is prohibited and may result in severe penalties, including a potential failing grade in the course. Be familiar with and adhere to academic integrity standards.
  • Re-Grading: If you spot grading errors, submit a re-grade request on Gradescope within a week of receiving your grade.
  • Announcements: Stay updated via lectures and Piazza. It’s crucial to follow announcements and meet deadlines.
