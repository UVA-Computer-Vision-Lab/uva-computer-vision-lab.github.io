Prospective Students

  • Prospective PhD students starting 2025: please apply to graduate programs in the department of computer science at UVA and indicate your interest in working with me in your statement. Feel free to email Prof. Zezhou Cheng (zc3bp@virginia.edu) your CV with "Prospective student" as the subject.
  • Current UVA graduate and undergraduate students: if you’re interested in working with us, please complete this form. We will review the form and contact you if there is a match. We are open to collaborations both inside and outside the department of computer science at UVA.
  • Visiting students and research interns at external institutes: if you’re looking to work on research with us, please complete this form. We reach out if there is a match.
